Project Overview
Huns is a "blog application" where people write and share their knowledge, experience and skills.

Most of today's blogs have poor quality content problems.At the end of the project, after passing the appropriate payment system and serious membership processes, she/he will gain the right to write and such features will contribute to the creation of quality content.

How I Started to Design:
  • After the project was decided, user feedback about current blogs was examined.(Twitter, Medium and other discussion platforms.)
  • Thanks to the feedback from the users, I made improvements in the features and design that I will present on the Blog.
  • First, I did Low Fidelity Wireframe and Medium Fidelity Wireframe design work. In this way, I quickly prepared the skeleton of the application and was able to test its navigation.
  • Finally, after the High Fidelity Wireframe was prepared, I tested the necessary scenarios and completed my design.
