Project Overview
An application where luxury and rare works of art are auctioned and exhibited.

It is a platform that aims to exhibit the works of artists all over the world in the 21st century. All artists who meet the conditions of participation we offer can exhibit and evaluate their works on this platform.

How I Started to Design:
  • The pain points were identified and examined. This is one of pain points "The inability of people who rely on their talent and art to include their works in auctions and exhibitions and to gain a monetary value."
  • I quickly created a Low Fidelity Wireframe using (Crazy Eights) pen and paper and designed my work using Figma. I used HTML, CSS (Bootstrap, SASS) technologies in the coding studies of this project.
